# Platform Overview

This platform is not intended to become a traditional CRM.

The goal is to build a multi-tenant Enterprise Intelligence & Operating System for holding companies, agencies, and service-based organizations.

The platform should unify:

- strategic planning
- operational execution
- workforce economics
- service architecture
- pricing intelligence
- KPI governance
- forecasting
- task orchestration
- SOP execution
- proposal generation
- CRM operations
- AI-powered monitoring and intelligence

The long-term goal is to create a centralized operational and decision-intelligence layer for the organization.

---

# Core Philosophy

The platform should not only store data.

It should:
- understand the business
- understand operational structure
- understand economics
- understand delivery
- understand forecasts
- understand KPIs
- understand risks
- detect anomalies
- monitor execution
- generate recommendations
- proactively warn management

Every operational activity should eventually become:
- measurable
- explainable
- traceable
- forecastable
- optimizable
- auditable

The platform should eventually evolve into an enterprise cognitive operating system.

# Target Organizations

The platform is primarily designed for:

- holding companies
- agencies
- consulting firms
- creative organizations
- service-based businesses
- multi-business-unit companies

The platform must support:
- multiple organizations
- multiple tenants
- multiple business units
- independent user permissions
- centralized executive visibility
- configurable operational structures

Each business unit may:
- have different services
- have different workflows
- have different economics
- have different teams
- have different KPIs
- have different operational models

But leadership should still have unified visibility and governance.

The platform architecture should remain:

- modular
- scalable

- auditable

- AI-ready
- enterprise-grade

The system should preserve strict separation between:

- workforce economics

- commercial pricing
- forecasting
- CRM execution
- incentives
- workflow orchestration
- proposal generation

# Commercial Calculator

Purpose:
Allow business development teams to generate commercial pricing safely and consistently.

- consume operational cost baselines
